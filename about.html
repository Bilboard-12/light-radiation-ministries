Prophetic Foundation: The Light Ray Vision

Our ministry's name and purpose were established by a direct word from the Lord, connecting us to the power of transformation and light (John 8:12).

On **10/8/2022** in the early morning, while I was awake, I was so scared. I heard a voice repeatedly saying: **"Light ray, Light ray, Light ray Christian Fellowship (Light Radiation C.F.). I am the light of the world."** This voice was confirmed by Scripture: **John 8:12**.

From slavery to freedom

From carrying the **yoke of sin** to freedom

From curses to blessing

The Builder's Testimony (Our Call to Spiritual Building)

The call to be **"the builders"** is directly tied to our mandate to build **His people spiritually**, reflecting the vision of light and transformation.

I met a man on the way—he didn't know me, and I didn't know him. I was actually going to buy iron sheets for the church. It wasn't a dream or vision; I was walking around 10:00 AM in the morning when he said to me:

**"God did not call you to build this physical house,"** *[while pointing to a very big house upstairs]* **"because such a building like this is nothing in the eyes of God. But He called you to build His people spiritually. That's what you have been called for, and God will bless you."**
